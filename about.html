The Engineering
Standard

Voltage & Current is a peer-reviewed technical publication dedicated to the advancement of electrical and electronics engineering. Founded in 2025, our mission is to provide a high-fidelity registry for research, field methodologies, and industrial analysis.

Peer Review

Every publication undergoes a rigorous validation process by our senior engineering board to ensure technical accuracy and implementation feasibility.

Open Access

We believe in the democratization of technical knowledge. Our registry is open to all qualified professionals and students globally.

Our Core Focus

We specialize in the documentation of power systems, renewable energy integration, industrial automation, and semiconductor advancements. By bridging the gap between theoretical research and field implementation, we empower engineers to build a more sustainable and efficient energy architecture.
